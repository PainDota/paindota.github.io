Introduction

Dota can be broken down into a few major principles. Some are specific to your role, many are general and apply to the game as a whole.

In our sessions, we’ll focus on deepening your understanding of these core principles. While replay analysis will be our primary tool, we’ll also use other effective methods to ensure your mastery is complete. Mastering these principles won’t just help you excel in your primary role—it will elevate your overall game sense. Over time, you’ll develop the ability to apply what you’ve learned to every hero and role, transforming your gameplay entirely.

The MMR will come as you apply each of these principles.

Principles

  • Laning
  • Farming Patterns
  • Itemization
  • How to approach team fights
  • Objectives (Roshan / Buildings)
  • Drafting your Hero
  • Extensive Laning Mechanics
  • Match-ups
  • Split Pushing
  • Playing the Map Properly
  • Item Timings
  • Hero Timings
  • Enemy Timings
  • Mental Approach
  • Warding
  • Rotations

I generally suggest we approach them in this order, but if you already mastered some of them we'll skip to the next ones

Aside from teaching the Dota fundamentals, I offer additional exclusives to students who have a more vested interest in gaining MMR.

    Not all questions can be answered in a coaching session. With this perk, you can ask me specific questions about your gam es without needing an additional session. I’m here to support your growth, and I’ll provide thoughtful answers and insights to help you improve, even between lessons.

    When it comes to gaining MMR, understanding the meta is key. Not everyone has the time to grind out games, watch pro matches , and study item builds. That’s why, with this addition, we’ll have a short session where I’ll teach you the meta early, giving you time to practice the right builds and maximize your MMR gains.

  • Match-up Guide

    Even if you understand all the principles, some things can only be learned with experience. With every new patch comes new match-ups to explore. You can watch this analysis later and break it down at your own pace, applying what you learn to improve.

You’ll get an hour-long (or more) session where I break down every micro and macro decision a pro makes and show you how to apply it to your own gameplay. Struggling with a broken meta hero?     I’ll personally add you to my friend list and keep a close eye on your progress. Think of me as looking over your shoulder throughout your improvement journey—stopping you from tilt queuing or picking the wrong heroes, and keeping you on track for consistent growth.
