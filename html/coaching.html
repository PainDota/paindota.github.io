Introduction

Dota can be broken down into a few major principles. Some of them are specific to your role, many of them are general and apply to the game as a whole.

In our sessions we will improve your understanding of all these principles. While this will primarily be done through replay review, there are many other methods that we can utilize to ensure that your understanding of these principals are complete. Mastering these principles will not only help you improve in your primary role, but your game sense overall. Eventually, your game sense will be developed to the point where you can apply the things you learn to all heroes in all roles.

The MMR will come as you apply each of these principles.

Principles

  • Laning
  • Farming Patterns
  • Itemization
  • How to approach team fights
  • Objectives (Roshan / Buildings)
  • Drafting your Hero
  • Extensive Laning Mechanics
  • Match-ups
  • Split Pushing
  • Playing the Map Properly
  • Item Timings
  • Hero Timings
  • Enemy Timings
  • Mental Approach

I generally suggest we approach them in this order, but if you already mastered some of them we'll skip to the next ones

Exclusive Content

Aside from teaching the fundamentals of DotA, I offer additional exclusive additions to students who have a more vested interest in gaining MMR.

Those who sign up for tiered coaching can obtain the perks mentioned below.

  • Discord Messaging

    Not all questions can be answered in a general fundamental video. With this perk you can ask me questions about your games. I teach many students so I may not always be able to get back to you immediately but I’ll try to answer questions that you might have in-between lessons.

  • Patch + Meta Explanation

    When it comes to gaining MMR, understanding the meta is key. Not everyone has the time to grind out the games, watch pro matches, and study item builds. With this addition we’ll have a short session where I teach you the meta early and give you time to practice the right builds in order to capitalize on your MMR gains.

  • Match-up Guide

    Even if you understand all the fundamentals, some things can’t be learned without experience. And with each new patch comes new match-ups to learn. With this perk we’ll have a short session where I teach you how to play specific matches you struggle with and save you from losing MMR as you learn the match up.

  • Replay Guide

    I like to keep my lessons targeted on the principles that I feel are important. If you want to learn all the things you’ve done wrong in a game, I’ll record an extra session where I record myself analyzing all the decisions you’ve made in an entire game. You can watch this back later and pick it apart at whatever pace you’d like.

  • Analysis Tutorial

    When it comes to your own improvement, obtaining the ability to analyze your own replays, as well as professional players will take you further than lessons. I’ll teach you how to dissect your own game-play and learn from the best. With this perk you won’t ever need to pay for another DotA coach again.

  • Training Plans

    Patterns of mistakes that require multiple replays to be reviewed can take up a lot of sessions to be fixed. Training Plans are the fix of that problem and side by side with Coaching Sessions, this will significantly speed up the improvement process. Click here for more details about the Plans.

  • Pro Player Analysis

    Analyzing and Learning from Pros is a headache and sometimes impossible for most players. This will fix that. It will be an hour-long or more session where I’ll go over every micro & macro decision-making of the Pro and how you can use it to your advantage. There’s a certain hero broken in the meta, but you don’t know how to play it? This solves it. Instead of playing 50 games on the said hero and losing 35/50, you can win 35/50.

  • Personal Progress Checkup

    I will personally add you to my friend list and look over your progress. I’ll look at your games and frequently message you item builds, broken meta heroes, and stuff that will get you ahead of everyone. I’ll be looking over your shoulder throughout your improvement journey, stopping you from tilt queuing or playing the wrong heroes.
