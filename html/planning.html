Service

  1. Student provides their Dotabuff, amount of games they can play in a week and their current behavior score.
  2. I will research their profile in-depth
    • a. Review countless replays
    • b. Analyze their match history
    • c. Track general stats and their hero pool
  3. Present a unique practice plan for the student to address core weaknesses found from my research that will help them improve their game-play and increase their ranks.

Comparison with Coaching

Coaching involves reviewing a replay and teaching a player to correct the mistakes they made in that game. When a coach only reviews one or two replays it's difficult to get a complete perspective on the student's overarching weaknesses. Some weaknesses may not even show up in specific games.

By looking at multiple replays and evaluating the student as a whole, I'm able to get a holistic view of the student and find weaknesses that are consistent across their entire profile.

Note: A training plan is not a complete substitute for coaching! While I try to address as many things as possible, there is some benefit that can only be had when the student engages in dialogue with a coach while watching their own replay.
